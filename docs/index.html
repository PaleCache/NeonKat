NeonKat

A simple music player with visualizer and miniplayer

The Best Local Music Player for Privacy & Aesthetics in 2026

NeonKat is a lightweight, privacy first local music player for Windows and Linux, the perfect choice when you want beautiful visualizers, offline playback of your own music collection, deep customization, and zero creepy tracking or telemetry.

Key Features of NeonKat Music Player

  • Playlist Loading & Saving Easily create, load, and save your music playlists so you never lose your perfect vibe.
  • Embedded & Folder Cover Art Support Automatically loads cover.png, cover.jpg, or cover.gif from the song folder for all tracks without artwork. You can also use song specific images (e.g. songname.png) for individual tracks perfect for custom album art.
  • Music Visualizer Stunning audio reactive visualizer that brings your music to life with smooth animations and beat sync effects.
  • Always On Top Mini Mode Compact floating player window that stays on top of other apps great for gaming, studying, or multitasking while listening.
  • Classic Music Player Controls Play, pause, next/previous, seek bar, volume slider, shuffle, repeat, and all the essentials you expect.
  • YouTube and Soundcloud downloads via yt-dlp Download videos, have them automatically converted to mp3 format with coverart embedded (requires yt-dlp + ffmpeg installed). Windows users: install easily with Scoop.
  • Internet Radio & Stream Support Listen to thousands of online radio stations and custom streams directly in the player.
  • Neon Mode: Beat reactive Colors The interface pulses and changes colors in sync with the music beat
  • Custom Picture in Picture Mode Watch music videos in a custom resizeable window
  • Custom Backgrounds Set any local image or even a URL as your player background make it match your desktop aesthetic perfectly.
  • Canvas Mode with Short MP4 Previews Display short looping video clips or full music videos as visual background in the visualizer canvas for maximum vibe.
  • Jellyfin Music Server Integration Stream your entire music library from a Jellyfin server local network or remote, access made easy.
  • Automatic Updates (disabled by default to protect your privacy.) Get the latest features and fixes automatically
  • Zero External Connections by Default Completely offline first design. Your music, playlists, and data never leave your device unless you choose to stream.

Built for people who love clean interfaces, deep customization, and privacy respecting music players with a neon / cyber aesthetic.
